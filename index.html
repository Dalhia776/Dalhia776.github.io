Remind Me

A rails app built for busy nurses to remind them of upcoming due dates for things such as paperwork and appointments. Sends out text message (via Twilio) and email (via SendGrid) reminders when 2 weeks, 1 week, and 2 days from due date. Uses Heroku scheduler to manage reminders being sent out daily.

You Can Do It!

A ruby on rails To Do app. I enjoyed building this and learning to work with different states of an object in the process. Great application to keep you from forgetting the important things in your day.
